Fix Pepper Drive!

The Fix Pepper Drive Association is a 501(c)3 nonprofit dedicated to improving the Pepper Drive community. We advocate for and facilitate:

  • Sidewalks, Paths, Crosswalks
  • Street Landscaping
  • Bike Lanes
  • Curbs
  • Drainage Improvements
  • Community Park Areas
  • Utility Improvements
